      How to Play Dong Dong

      Objective

      The goal is to accurately predict and bid on the number of "stacks" you will win in each round. Points are awarded for correct bids.

      This game is to be played with 4 players. The deck consists of 52 tiles, 4 suites (colors) of tiles 1 though 13.

      Rounds

      In each round, players are dealt a number of tiles equal to the round number. There is a maximum of 13 rounds.

      Betting

      At the start of each round, players place a bet on how many stacks they believe they will win. The total number of bets from all players cannot equal the round number.

      Gameplay

      • Master Color: A "Master Color" is chosen for each stack. This is the color that will trump all others for that round.
      • Leading a Stack: The first player of a stack sets the "lead color".
      • Following Suit: Other players must play a tile of the same color if they have one. If not, they can play any tile.
      • Winning a Stack: The stack is won by the player who played the highest lead color tile, unless trumped by a master color tile. In this case, the highest master color tile wins.

      Scoring

      If your bet matches the number of stacks you won, you get 10 + (your bet)^2 points.

      If your bet is incorrect, you lose (your bet - stacks won)^2 points.
